    Instruction
    This map shows the information of bars in Glasgow city, and the data collected from Google Map and bars' menus.

    This map contains 7 functions：

    1.Mouse over the points on the map will popup the name, clicking will show its specific information;

    2.Clicking on the search button will bring up the search bar;

    3.Hovering the 'selection' button will bring up several checkboxes. The default is unchecked. And if you check one of them， the map will only display the bars offer the corresponding item;

    4.Click the 'reviews' button and a slider bar will pop up in the top left corner of the map. dragging the knob will filter the bars above a certain rating. Clicking other buttons will remove the pop-up. And this function can be used together with function 3；

    5.Clicking the 'map pin' button and then clicking on a location on the map will mark that location. And clicking on the exist marker icon can cancel the marker.

    6.Click on 'full screen' for full screen display on most browsers, exit by pressing 'ESC' on the keyboard or click on the 'full screen' button again.

    7.The map supports 'dark' mode. When switching to 'dark' mode, the interface style and map style will change.
